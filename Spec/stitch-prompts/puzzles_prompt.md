# Puzzles Prompt

## Base Prompt

Create a responsive chess puzzle product concept for a web app with two surfaces:

- a puzzle bank
- a puzzle trainer

The bank should help users understand:

- due items
- ownership
- difficulty
- themes

The trainer should feel:

- focused
- coach-like
- clear about status
- clear about hints, reveal, retry, and solution playback states

Keep the visual language aligned with the same product family as the landing page and workbench.

Prioritize:

- trust
- clarity
- fast scanning
- a strong board area

Avoid:

- gamified noise
- excessive badges
- arcade-style visuals
- too much visual clutter

This should feel like serious study software, not a puzzle game app.

## Refinement Prompts

### Refinement 1

Make due items and difficulty easier to scan in the bank.

### Refinement 2

Make the trainer feel more instructional and less game-like.

### Refinement 3

Show clearer hint, reveal, and retry states.

### Refinement 4

Reduce visual noise and keep the board area dominant.

## Success Check

The result is good if:

- users can scan what is due and what matters
- the trainer feels focused and instructional
- hint and retry states are easy to understand
- the design still feels like the same app family as landing and workbench
